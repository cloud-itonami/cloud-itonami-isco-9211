# Business Model: Independent Crop Farm Labour Practice

## Classification

- Repository: `cloud-itonami-isco-9211`
- ISCO-08: `9211`
- Occupation: Crop Farm Labourers
- Social impact: food-security, rural-livelihoods, worker-safety

## Customer

- smallholder farms
- farm co-ops
- seasonal harvest operations

## Offer

- planting support
- weeding assist
- harvest-carrying support
- safety-brief logging
- reporting

## Revenue

- per-job fee
- monthly labour-crew platform fee
- reporting package

## Trust Controls

- no robot operation near heavy equipment without governor gate
- treatment application near water sources requires human sign-off
- labour and safety records are auditable, not editable
